Duck Hall of Fame: Celebrities of the Century

Daffy Duck

Daffy Duck is from Looney Toons and Merrie Melodies and has a love-hate relationship with the infamous Bugs Bunny.

Donald Duck

Donald Duck is an iconic, short-tempered Disney character who has made a wide range of appearances in comics and TV.

Daisy Duck

Daisy Duck is a tempermental, but sweet, Disney character that is known for being Donald Duck's girlfriend.

Scrooge McDuck

Scrooge McDuck, Donald Duck's rich uncle, is a Disney Character who is smart, logical, and cares a lot for his family.

World's Largest Rubber Duck

6 stories high and 74 feet long, this duck is one you'll definitely need a larger bathtub for.

CS50 Duck

This duck is designed to help coders work through their problems by formulating their ideas in a sort of conversation.
